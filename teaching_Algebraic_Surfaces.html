Algebraic Surfaces

Summer Semester 2024

Lecturer

  • Dr. Massimiliano Alessandro

Time and Place

  • Time: Wednesday and Thursday, 12:00-14.00.
  • Room: Gebäude E2 4 - Seminarraum 6.

Note: The course will be held in English.


Content

The principal aim of this course is to achieve the so-called Enriques-Kodaira classification of complex algebraic surfaces.

Prerequisites: Linear Algebra I & II, Analysis I & II & III, Complex Analysis (Funktionentheorie).


Registration

To register for the course, please send an email to malessandro [add @math.uni-sb.de]. This email should include also the following data: Name, Surname and Matrikelnummer.


Exams

Exam criteria: oral exam at the end of the course.
